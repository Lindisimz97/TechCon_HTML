TechCon 2024, scheduled for April 12 at NYU Stern, will explore the theme "AI Demystified: From Buzz to Business." The event aims to move beyond the hype surrounding AI, focusing on its practical applications in business. Attendees can expect discussions on AI’s impact on jobs, ethical concerns, and emerging startups in the AI space. Industry experts from companies like Google, Microsoft, and IBM will offer their insights, making it a must-attend event for students, professionals, and entrepreneurs interested in AI-driven innovation​( NYU Stern School of Business ).
